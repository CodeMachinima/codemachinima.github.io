Glitched

Image 1: For these glitched images, I choose a vocaloid named Kagamine Rin, and I used text editing on the image for each. I really like this vocaloid and mixed with rock music is like heaven to me. I really enjoy glitching this image, it really fits her character but also kind of fun for me

Image 2,3, and 4: For my series of glitched images, I choose all to be related to a genre of music I like, which is any kind of rock music. I prefer more of the ones that are hard rock, pop rock, metalcore, j-rock, or emo. I just like the distortion of the music and sometimes can be really emotional. For my first image, I used text edit to modify the image, it was kinda hard to make it into what I like since if I keep copy and pasting something, it will destroy the image, I also just added a bunch of letters just to see what happens. The image is a metalcore band named Bring Me The Horizon from 2006. For the second image, I used audacity, using the echo effect and the distortion effect with the fades in order to have a colorful effect.  The image is a rock band named Panic! At the Disco from 2005. The last image, I used audacity again, applied a studio fade out, echos, and a delay, which created this colorful glitched image. The image is a mascot for a vocaloid rock artist Neru-P. I choose these images becuase I listen to their music a lot.
