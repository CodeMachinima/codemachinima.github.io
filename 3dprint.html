Here is my 3D print of my Minecraft model, Dokibird, printed on a Flashforge Adventurer 5M Pro. I printed it really big, and it took really long to print (around 7 hours). Taking off the supports was easy, but a little tedious, especially when it left white spots. I took out the white spots by using my roommate's tea kettle, with the steam coming out of the kettle. It was able to make the discoloration a bit gone, but not by a lot. In the future, I would probably use a lighter carefully and place it under the model. Overall, I really like how the model came out, the black filament gave it a shiny and pretty look, and I like how it is presented. Unfortunately, it's not painted, but I think it's better that way.
