Halo Machinima

Machinima is using a gaming engine or a video game itself to create a cinematic film out of it, one example is Red VS. Blue. I want to create a Halo machinima parodying debates and creating a whole story around it. The story will be comedic and I want to try and just have fun with the project. The game I used is Halo Reach (from Halo MCC) to create my project. I use the theatre mode feature + free camera + voice acting to create the film, not just showcasing gameplay. The video editing I use is Final Cut Pro.
